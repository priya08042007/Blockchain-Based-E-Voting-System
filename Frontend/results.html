Election Results Overview

The election results panel displays the final vote count for each candidate based on booth-wise voting data.

How Results Are Calculated

  • Votes are recorded securely on the blockchain
  • Each vote is immutable and tamper-proof
  • Counting is fully automated and transparent

Booth-Based Results

Administrators can view results booth-wise by entering a valid Booth ID. This helps in localized verification and auditing.

Transparency & Integrity

Since all votes are stored on blockchain, results cannot be altered once voting ends, ensuring fair elections.

Admin Responsibility

The admin monitors result declaration and ensures accurate, unbiased publication of election outcomes.
