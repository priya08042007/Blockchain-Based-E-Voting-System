About Political Parties

Political parties are organized groups of individuals who share common ideologies and contest elections to form governments or represent citizens in legislative bodies.

Purpose of Adding Parties

  • Registers recognized political parties
  • Links candidates with party identity
  • Ensures clarity during voting and result display

Party Identity

Each party is uniquely identified by its name, leader, and logo. This identity helps voters easily recognize parties on the ballot.

Blockchain Registration

Party details are securely stored on the blockchain, ensuring immutability and preventing unauthorized changes.

Role of Party Logo

  • Helps voters visually identify parties
  • Improves accessibility for less-literate voters
  • Displayed during voting and result declaration

Admin Responsibility

The administrator must ensure that party information is accurate and verified before registration to maintain election integrity.
