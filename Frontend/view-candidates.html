About Candidates

Candidates are individuals who contest in elections to represent voters in democratic institutions. Their details must be accurately recorded to ensure a fair and transparent election process.

Purpose of Candidate Listing

  • Displays registered candidates for verification
  • Ensures transparency in election preparation
  • Helps administrators audit candidate data

Candidate Verification

All listed candidates are verified by the election administrator before being stored on the blockchain.

Blockchain Integrity

Candidate records are immutable and tamper-proof once registered.

Polling Booth Mapping

  • Each candidate is linked to a specific booth
  • Ensures correct vote allocation
  • Prevents misrepresentation
