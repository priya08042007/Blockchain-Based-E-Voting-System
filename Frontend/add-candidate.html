About Candidates

Candidates are individuals who contest in elections to represent voters in democratic institutions. Their details must be accurately recorded to ensure a fair and transparent election process.

Purpose of Adding Candidates

  • Registers eligible contestants for the election
  • Ensures clear identification of candidates
  • Links candidates with their political parties

Candidate Verification

Before adding a candidate, their identity and eligibility must be verified by the election administrator to prevent fraudulent entries.

Blockchain Record

Once added, candidate details are stored on the blockchain. This ensures immutability and prevents unauthorized modification.

Polling Booth Mapping

  • Each candidate is associated with a polling booth
  • Ensures booth-wise vote allocation
  • Prevents misrouting of votes

Admin Responsibility

The administrator is responsible for maintaining accurate candidate data. Incorrect entries may affect election results.
