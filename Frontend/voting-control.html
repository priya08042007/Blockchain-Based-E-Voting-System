About Voting

Voting is the cornerstone of a democratic society. It provides citizens with the right and responsibility to choose their representatives and influence public decision-making.

Importance of Voting

  • Ensures citizen participation in governance
  • Strengthens democratic legitimacy
  • Holds elected representatives accountable

Blockchain-Based Voting

Blockchain technology ensures decentralization, immutability, and transparency. Every vote is recorded as a transaction and secured using cryptographic hashing.

Security Features

  • End-to-end encryption of votes
  • Immutable ledger prevents tampering
  • Eliminates duplicate or fake votes

Voter Privacy

Voter identity is separated from vote data. While votes are verifiable, voter anonymity is strictly preserved.

Auditability

Election authorities and auditors can verify vote counts without accessing voter identities, ensuring transparency and trust.

Remote & Inclusive Voting

  • Enables participation for migrants
  • Accessible to elderly and disabled voters
  • Reduces dependency on physical polling booths
